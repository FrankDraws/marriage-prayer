Marriage Prayer

Hug, kiss her on the forehead, look her in the eyes, and say:

Babe I love you. I'm so happy that I get to be your husband.

FATHER I thank you for this beautiful woman that you gave to me as a gift.

I pray that you bless her day today and let her experience your presence in a real way.

Challenge her to grow.

And FATHER, teach me to be the man that she needs.

Teach me to reflect your love and your passion for her in a real way.

And may I only ever speak over her, what you say about her.

Thank you FATHER for the honor of getting to be her husband.
